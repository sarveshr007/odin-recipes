Chicken Tikka Masala is a creamy, spiced Indian dish featuring tender marinated chicken in a rich tomato-based curry, perfect for a flavorful dinner.

Key Ingredients

Marinate

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1 tsp indian spice called garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • salt to taste

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp coriander
  • salt to taste and fresh cilantro for granish

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, chili powder, and salt in a bowl. Add chicken pieces, coat well, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  2. Cook the Chicken: Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Skewer the marinated chicken and grill or bake for 15-20 minutes until slightly charred, turning halfway. Alternatively, cook in a hot skillet for 5-7 minutes per side.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat. Sauté the onion until golden, about 5 minutes. Add tomato puree, garam masala, cumin, coriander, and salt. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  4. Combine and Simmer: Add the cooked chicken to the sauce, stir in the heavy cream, and simmer for 5-7 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  5. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
